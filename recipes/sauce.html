How to Make a Gyoza Sauce

Ingredients and Prep

  • (1/2 cup) rice vinegar
  • (1/2 cup) soy sauce
  • (1) green onion: thinly sliced
  • (1) garlic clove: minced
  • (1 teaspoon) sesame oil
  • (1/2 teaspoon) crushed red pepper flakes
  • (1/2 teaspoon) ginger: minced

Instructions

  1. Whisk all the ingredients together in a bowl. Let sit for 15 minutes before serving.
  2. Sauce is safe to consume for up a 1 week if stored covered in the fridge.
